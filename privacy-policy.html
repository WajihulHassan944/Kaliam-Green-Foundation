Our Privacy Policy

Kaliam Green Foundation values your privacy and is committed to safeguarding the personal information you provide. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information you voluntarily share with us through:

  • Our website
  • Email, mail, phone calls, or event registrations
  • Advocacy efforts, reply forms, or other communication channels

Additionally, when you visit our website, we collect non-personal data about your computer hardware and software for analytics purposes. Cookies are used temporarily to maintain session data, such as shopping cart contents, but no personal data is stored or shared through cookies unless you voluntarily provide it.

Use of Personal Information

We use your personal information to:

  1. Provide the services you request.
  2. Comply with applicable laws and regulations.
  3. Protect our intellectual property and legal rights.
  4. Expand our audience and donor base by exchanging limited contact details with like-minded organizations. You can opt out of these exchanges at any time by contacting our Donor Services Department.

We may engage third-party service providers for tasks like data processing, marketing, analytics, hosting, billing, and fraud protection. These providers are contractually obligated to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Managing Your Information

You may access, update, correct, or delete the information you have provided by contacting our Donor Services Department. To unsubscribe from emails or other communications, follow the instructions included in each message or contact us directly.

Children Under Age 18

Kaliam Green Foundation does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 18. If you are under 18, please do not submit information without the consent of a parent or guardian.

Our Commitment to Data Security

We use advanced encryption technologies to protect your personal and financial information. As an added layer of security, we do not store credit card details.
